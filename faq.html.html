Frequently Asked Questions

1Q. I have a question on exam syllabus how to contact?

Please write an email with your details and question to exams@swarabharathi.com

2Q. I have registered but not paid the exam fee, How can I proceed further?

Contact us on exams@swarabharathi.com we will help on the payment and completion of registration process.

3Q. What ID proofs are valid for exam?

Any ID proof issued by local Govt is valid. ID proof must your Photo, Name and Date of Birth. For example (India: Aadhar Card, Driving Licence, Voter ID, PAN Card, Passport etc).

4Q.I have registered and paid the fee, but after checking syllabus I am feeling I am not ready for Exam need some more time.

Please write an email (exams@swarabharathi.com)to us we will reschedule for Exam.

5Q.Is Swara Bharathi affiliated to any university?

NO, Not yet we are working on it.

6Q. I have written exam, but not really happy with my result, can I retake the exam?

YES, you can retake the same exam within one month, without paying any addition Fee. Please write an email (exams@swarabharathi.com)to us with your details, including marks memo return.

7Q. I would like to get marks memo hard copy, is it possible?

Yes, we can postal mail the printed memo, you have to pay postal/courier Charges.

8Q. I have misspelled my details in registrations, How can I correct it?

Please write us (exams@swarabharathi.com), we can correct before the memos are sent. There will be addition charges after the memo is

9Q. Is there any age limit for writing the Exam?

. Minimum age limit is 8years and there is no upper age limit.

10Q.I have not received registration email, memo, what can I do?

Please write your details to exams@swarabharathi.com we will help you.

11Q. I have paid the Fee, but not ready for exam can get a fee return?

NO, payment can not be return. You can only request for extent of the exam schedule.

12Q. Before the exam, can I take demo/sample exam?

YES, we have a free exam you can take without paying any Fee.

13Q. I have learning Music for long, can I directly write higher grades?

YES, you can write any grade directly at your risk.

14Q. I have received the memo, i wanted a printed certificate.
